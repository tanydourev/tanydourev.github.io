☕ KOFI typeface

KOFI is a monoline display typeface inspired by the Kofi Aromo coffee franchise in Activision Blizzard's Overwatch. The original set of letterforms, which were featured as part of the Kofi Aromo logo, were expanded to a full alphabet, numbers and punctuation using shapes and curves that were prominent throughout the lettering itself. Given the coffee franchise and its associated logo were created for the sole purpose of set-dressing the in-game world of Numbani, I saw this as a fun opportunity to explore my love of typography and touch on the potential Kofi Aromo had for becoming a brand.
