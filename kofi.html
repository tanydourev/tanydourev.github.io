Typeface

KOFI is a monoline display typeface inspired by the Kofi Aromo coffee franchise in Activision Blizzard's Overwatch and Overwatch 2 titles.
KOFI's existence came about after I discovered the Kofi Aromo logo and watermark while playing the Numbani map in the first edition of Overwatch. Originally designed to set-dress the environment, the brand is marketed as an international coffee franchise with additional locations in Ayutthaya, Ilios, Oasis, Sydney, Toronto and Samoa. In Numbani, players can move through the cafe's physical location, but are unable to interact with it.
My interest in Kofi Aromo stemmed from the logo, which I believed had unused potential as simply a set piece for Numbani. Using the letterforms and shapes that already existed, I created a full uppercase alphabet, a complete numberset and punctuation.
