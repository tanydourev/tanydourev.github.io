THE CHALLENGE

RW Digital's analytics showed decent engagement via its website and a positive reputation through previous client work, but the agency's online presence and brand identity needed strengthening.

Prior to joining the agency, RW Digital already had a partially-established brand identity; after an audit with the agency principal, we discovered that the application of the guidelines was inconsistent and noted areas for improvement. Given the agency's main channels of communication are through its website and social media, we devised a goal to both improve the brand identity and make better use of social platforms to extends its reach.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Pulling the IG grid out of the toolbox.

One of the key goals with the brand refresh was to enact a new strategy for the agency's social media networks, supplemented by the new branding guidelines. Instead of relying just on the content, we experimented with using the Instagram grid layout to create a visually-appealing look for viewers. Our first strategy was to change the colour theme of posts every two months, shifting to the next hue in the spectrum and using organic shapes in the background.

While this strategy was successful, we found that it became difficult to connect the post themes to the colours outlined in the branding guidelines. After a second assessment, we created a new set of requirements for social media graphics:

AGENCY WEBSITE

The all-in-one go-to for services.

Alongside the agency Principal, I was responsible for creating a new design for the agency website and apply the new branding guidelines to its structure. Given the agency's main channels of communication are through its website and social media, we devised a goal to both improve the brand identity and make better use of the website to extends its reach. We expanded the website to include service pages for the regions the agency serviced, simplified content to make it easier to understand for viewers, and performed usability checks for responsiveness and accessibility adherence. Additionally, we used language and copy that is easy to understand, encouraging and simplified ‚Äî all while maintaining a level of professionalism and expertise that marks RW Digital as a leader in the Canadian digital marketing industry.

VISUAL UI

Approachable and modern.

The styling of the agency website reflects the new branding guidelines, making use of Poppins as the primary typeface and a colour palette comprised of whites/blue/turquoise. Illustrations are hand-selected from the Cuate collection, depicting digital marketing and analytics scenes. We selected this visual style for its combination of playfulness and modernity, embracing the agency's adaptable nature to the shifting trends in digital marketing.
