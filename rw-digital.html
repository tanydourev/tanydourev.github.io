RW DIGITAL INTERNSHIP

2021-22 / Graphic Design, Social Media / Figma, Adobe Illustrator

Working alongside RW Digital, a digital marketing agency in Richmond, British Columbia, I've had the pleasure of delving into the world of digital marketing. My responsibilities have included managing the agency's social media feeds through well-designed posts that promote both the agency and digital marketing practices; creating branding content; strengthening the agency's branding style through a revamp of their current assets. This has been a wonderful opportunity to experience first-hand the working world of graphic design and marketing for social media platforms.

↓

01/ SOCIAL POSTS

This includes content primarily for Instagram post (carousel) and story, as well as paid posts/stories and banners for Wordpress. The agency's feed was also incorporated as part of the brand identity, with colour themes changing every few months to emphasize fluidity and acceptance of change.

02/ BRANDING

I was also given the oppportunity to work on a business card design for the agency's owner. This design was later incorporated in additional branding elements, such as digital posts and print content. Notably, RW Digital's print ad can be found in the March/April 2022 edition of the What's On Richmond magazine.
