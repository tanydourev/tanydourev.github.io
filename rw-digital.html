Graphic Design & Social Media
My internship at RW Digital, a digital marketing agency in British Columbia, provided me with the opportunity to delve into digital marketing for web, SEO optimization and management, and social media marketing. Examples of responsibilities include: creating content for the agency's social media platforms to promote digital marketing practices; strengthening branding identity through use of guidelines and brand elements; designing print content for business cards and advert placements; providing a fresh perspective on using social media for marketing.
SOFTWARE USED :
Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Wordpress
