Overview

RW Digital is a digital marketing agency based in Richmond, British Columbia. I joined the team in 2021 as a Graphic Designer, growing the agency through social media marketing, revamped branding strategies and supporting website creation for clients.
Empowering businesses with digital marketing — one post, one website, one strategy at a time.

Marketing

Prior to joining the agency, RW Digital already had an established visual and branding identity for its graphical assets. The goal with my involvement was to emphasize the branding standards for social media and help grow the agency's online presence. Over the course of a year, I collaborated alongside the agency Principal to experiment with various combinations of colours and arrangements for posts, as well as utilize the Instagram feed as a tool to visually unify content. It was important to find a balance between visually-engaging content and accessibility, as most viewers access social media platforms through mobile and tablet applications.
Since becoming part of the team, I've created posts for topics such as educational content on digital marketing (tutorials, trends, industry updates); testimonials and client feedback for services; holidays and community events; and showcases for client projects.
To view more of my designs for RW Digital, visit the agency's Instagram page at @digital.rw.




Client Websites

As part of RW Digital's analytics and SEO performance services, I am tasked with designing client websites through WordPress and creating graphic assets to showcase finished products. When redesigning a website, the workflow begins with a visual consultation with the agency Principal to identify necessary changes and usability issues. Existing content is transferred to the new website, then stylized according to client branding guidelines and accessibility standards.

Agency Branding

Through social media marketing and re-designing the agency website, I clarified the branding guidelines for RW Digital and established a standard for all content moving forward. The agency aims to present itself as credible and informative with its services, while simultaneously encouraging a friendly and welcoming approach to digital marketing. Graphical content relies strongly on patterned backgrounds, gradients, and marketing/business illustrations; testimonials are designed to creatively represent clients and their fields of work. The agency website features similar layouts for groupings of pages, a strict colour palette based on the agency logo, and is routinely checked for accessibility.
