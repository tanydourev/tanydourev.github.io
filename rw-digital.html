RW DIGITAL is a digital marketing agency based in Richmond, British Columbia. As a graphic designer within their team, I am responsible for creating social media graphics for services provided, educational posts on digital marketing, client project showcases and events in the Richmond community. Additionally, I collaborate with the agency Principal on client websites and strengthen the agency's brand identity through marketing material.

An important value of RW Digital as a brand is to acknowledge the history and traditions of the Asian community in Richmond. For major holidays such as the Mid-Autumn Festival and Lunar New Year, I have the pleasure of creating graphics that bring to light the beauty of these events.

Since joining the agency, I have worked on website designs for RW Digital, the Richmond Heart of AIDS Society , and the Vancouver Island Self-Storage Association - with more to come in the future. RW Digital also received an upgraded brand identity in 2023, solidifying its existence as a leader in digital marketing services within Canada.
