VINNA was born out of a love for taking product concepts to the next idea through 3D modelling. I was interested in developing a cosmetics brand with a custom product design, designed fully within Blender and captured through simulatory product photography. The biggest challenge was learning the fundamentals of lighting used in commercial product photography, in addition to creating textures that were as photorealistic as possible within the bounds of the software. Overall, this was one of my favourite passion projects to date! ^-^
