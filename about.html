Meet Tany

Tany Dourev is an interaction designer, gamer & type nerd based in Canada. Her creative journey began with graphic/visual design, and has since expanded into typography, UX/UI design, 3D and game design. Much of her work is inspired by cyberpunk aesthetics, futuristic art and video games. Currently, she works as a Graphic Designer @ RW Digital Marketing Agency.

Outside of design, she enjoys a good cup of tea, watching Star Wars, listening to ambient music and travelling through her homeland of Bulgaria ✨
