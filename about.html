Hello there!

My name is Tany; I'm a Canada-based interaction and visual designer. Nice to meet you! ✨
I've studied interaction design at two institutions, graduating with a Bachelor of Arts and a specialization in digital prototyping. I currently work as a Graphic Designer at RW Digital, a Canadian digital marketing agency based in BC.
My creative journey began with graphic design during my teenage years. Since then, I've broadened my knowledge of the industry and stepped into fields like branding, UX/UI, design research, 3D design and game dev. I like to combine mediums and see projects span from conception to production, often taking on multiple roles as a one-woman show to see the process through to the very end. I'm a huge stickler for details; if I don't fix that stray pixel, I won't stop noticing it.
Outside of design, I enjoy exploring video game worlds, watching Star Wars' Andor show and discovering new ambient tunes (and re-coding my portfolio a million times, but we don't talk about that one). I'm a green tea girlie all the way; jasmine, peppermint, regular green, I'm always down for a warm cuppa. My favourite video games are CD Projekt RED's Cyberpunk 2077, BlueTwelve Studio's Stray and Quantic Dream's Detroit: Become Human.
