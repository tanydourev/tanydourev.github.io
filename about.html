Hi, I'm Tany!

I'm a interaction designer, gamer and type nerd based in Canada. While my creative journey began with graphic design, my interests have since expanded into typography, branding, UX/UI design and 3D design. Much of my work is inspired by video games, neo-futuristic art and cyberpunk aesthetics. Anything I create, I try to bring it past surface level and take advantage of its full potential by merging various design discplines together to create truly unique projects.

Outside of design, I enjoy a good cup of tea, watching F1 cars race around circuits and listening to ambient music from video game soundtracks ✨
