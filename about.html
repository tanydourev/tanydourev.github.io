✨ Get to know Tany

Hello there! I'm a Canada-based designer with a love for type and an avid gamer. My creative journey started with graphic design and has expanded into other fields of the industry, such as UX/UI, branding, 3D work and game design. A lot of my work is inspired by the idea of merging disciplines and pushing the boundaries of design — I create to take it the extra mile.


I graduated from Brock University with a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Arts & Sciences. Prior to my degree, I studied Interaction Design at the Sheridan College Institute of Technology.


Outside of design, I like a good cup of tea while watching Star Wars' Andor series or listening to ambient music. Some of my favourite games include Cyberpunk 2077, Detroit: Become Human and Stray.
