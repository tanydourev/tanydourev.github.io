Hi, I’m Tany! I’m a freelance interaction & visual designer based in Canada. Specializing in designing fun user experiences through prototyping and exploring new solutions to creative problems. A one-woman show, characterized by intense attention to detail and a goal to always be learning.

Currently working as a Graphic Designer @ RW Digital.

Enjoys exploring video game worlds, drinking green tea, listening to ambient tunes and re-coding her portfolio constantly. Recommends playing Cyberpunk 2077, Stray and Detroit: Become Human. Looking forward to season two of Star Wars’ Andor series. Trinket-collector and waffle-lover.
