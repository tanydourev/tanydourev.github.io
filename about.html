Hi, I'm Tany!

I'm a multimedia designer and type nerd with a love for fonts and video games. I began my creative journey as a graphic designer and have since expanded into typography, branding, UX/UI design and 3D design.

Much of my work is inspired by video games, neo-futuristic art and cyberpunk aesthetics. Anything I create, I try to bring it past surface level and take advantage of its full potential by merging various design discplines together to create truly unique projects.

Outside of design, I enjoy a good cup of tea and watching F1 cars race around circuits on the weekend.
