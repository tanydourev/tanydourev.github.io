Meet Tany.

Tany Dourev is an interaction designer and type nerd with a love for fonts and video games. She began her creative journey as a graphic designer and has since expanded into typography, interface design and 3D design. Much of her inspiration stems from video game worlds, neo-futuristic art on Pinterest and cyberpunk aesthetics. Outside of design, she enjoys a good cup of green tea and likes to watch cars go around circuits on the weekends.
