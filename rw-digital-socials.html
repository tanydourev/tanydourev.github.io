Overview

In 2021, I joined RW Digital's marketing and design team as a Graphic Designer. For over three years, my role has helped the agency grow through social media marketing, revamped branding strategies and supporting website creation for clients. Collaborating alongside the agency principal, I play a key role in the agency's in-house design team.

Empowering businesses with digital marketing — one post, one website, one strategy at a time.

Brand Evolution

Prior to joining the agency, RW Digital already had an existing visual and brand identity for its graphical assets. The goal with my involvement was to emphasize the branding standards for social media and help grow the agency's online presence. Over the course of a year, I worked with the agency principal to experiment with various combinations of colours and arrangements for posts; our goal was to identify oppprtunities to utilize the Instagram feed as a tool to visually unify content. It was important to find a balance between visually-engaging content and accessibility, as much of the content posted by the agency is text-based.


Through my role as a designer, I produce engaging multimedia graphics for the following types of content: digital marketing (tutorials, trends, industry updates); testimonials and client feedback for services; holidays and yearly celebrations; community events and sponsorships; and showcases for client projects. Graphical content relies strongly on patterned backgrounds, gradients, and marketing/business illustrations. Testimonials are designed to creatively represent clients and their fields of work. Posts that feature educational content on digital marketing utilize shades of blue, separating them from content that is for events, holidays or agency work.
