CYBERPSYCHO SIGHTING:
LIKE BROTHER, LIKE SISTER

2021 / Level Design / Unity, ProBuilder

“Cyberpsycho Sighting: Like Brother, Like Sister” is a cyberpsycho level designed to be integrated into CD Projekt Red's Cyberpunk 2077. Intended to be an exploration into the fundamentals of level design, it provides a unique set of gameplay challenges that emphasize vertical height and the unpredictability of cyberpsychos. Alongside a custom cyberpsycho character, storyline and lore details have also been crafted to accompany the Unity build.

01/ BLOCKOUT

A level blockout was designed using Unity and the ProBuilder plugin to visualize the level in a three-dimensional space. Ideally, the cyberpsycho level would take place within a shipping container storage or warehouse, as this makes up a majority of the buildings within Maelstrom gang terrority (Watson). 02/ CYBERPSYCHO

“Cyberpsycho” is a term used by the citizens of Night City to describe those that have developed psychopathic tendencies as a result of continuously enhancing and augmenting their bodies with cybernetics. They are affected by cyberpsychosis, a dissociative disorder that makes them dangerous to anyone who crosses their path. In Cyberpunk 2077, cyberpsycho levels are common and of higher difficulty for players, forcing them to utilize their V's skillsets to defeat an enemy.

NAME: Sazume Akuhara
GENDER: Female
AGE: 26 years
NATIONALITY: Japanese
FAMILY: Norio “Akki” Akuhara (brother), Mother (deceased), Father (unknown)
STATUS: Single
ABILITIES: Mantis blades (military-grade, manufactured by Arasaka Corporation), reinforced tendons (grants double jump ability)
AFFILIATIONS: Tyger Claws (former)

BIOGRAPHY: Born in the slums of Japantown, Sazume Akuhara was raised by a single mother while her older brother, Norio, went off to become a Buddhist scholar. She was later taken into the Tyger Claws gang under the protection of leader Sota Saito, who granted her immunity as a favour to Norio. After Saito's unfortunate death in an uprising, Sazume attemped to leave the Tyger Claws; this action was eventually deemed an act of betrayal and a warrant for her capture was made available to all gangs in Night City. After five months of being on the run, a Maelstrom squad caught a glimpse of her breaking into one of their storage warehouses and took her hostage. Sazume was physically tortured in the hopes of revealling details about the Tyger Claws' inner workings, but Maelstrom were unable to make her talk and she was handed back to the gang in a heavily-injured state. The Tyger Claws continued to keep Sazume a prisoner after her arrival, but avoided harming her under the orders of her brother. During this time, Norio had become a high-ranking member, but would eventually lose his role after his partner was killed and he slipped into cyberpsychosis state. With his vengeful murder spree redirecting the attention away from Sazume, she used the opportunity to escape her confines and flee across the city to seek asylum outside of Japantown. As the mental trauma from her past caught up to her, she slowly began experiencing symptoms of cyberpsychosis until the only thought in her mind was getting revenge on Maelstrom for their abuse towards her. Fueled by anger, she now spends her time hunting down gang members and leading them to gruesome deaths.

03/ QUEST DETAILS

Cyberspsycho levels are assigned by fixer Regina Jones. Players will receive a phone call from her when they near an area with a cyberpsycho. Ideally, players must use non-lethal means to neutralize a cyberpscho, however ammo-based weaponry is still accepted as long as a final blow is not dealt.

GIG TYPE: Cyberpsycho Sighting
QUEST GIVER: Regina Jones
AREA: Northside docks (Watson District)
REQUIREMENTS: Completed "The Rescue" (main job), "Cyberpsycho Sighting: Phantom of Night City (gig)
REWARDS: Player XP (257), Street Cred (623), €$2,748

MISSION DESCRIPTION: There’s a saying that a woman is a man’s greatest weakness, but I have a feeling that blade-wielding maniacs are far more dangerous when you're not watching your back. Think you can go deal with this one without getting chopped into pieces?

OBJECTIVES: Travel to the Northside docks, find the entrance to the warehouse, neutralize Sazume Akuhara, search the area to collect information, send a message to Regina about your findings.

Details about the quest are provided to players via phone calls and text messages.

“V, need you to do a little digging for me in one of the warehouses down by the docks. An NCPD case opened up last week on a lost shipment of military-grade mantis blades meant for Arasaka. Bad news is they’re currently in the hands of Sazuma Akuhara, sister of the same Akuhara you tracked down in Corpo Plaza. Word has it she’s gone off the rails like her brother, so see what you can find without getting your head sliced off.”

REGINA: Forgot to mention, the warehouse used to be a storage facility for shipping containers until OTEC did a major recall and cancelled their production. They never made it back to the factory for recycling, so they’ve been gathering dust ever since. Not sure how stable they’ll be if you end up doing any climbing, so be careful.

REGINA: Find anything yet?

V: Turns out our little friend is both the reason the shipment disappeared and Maelstrom gang members have been dropping like flies lately. She intercepted the transport, took the blades and installed them on herself instead of going to a ripperdoc because she knew they would get flagged as stolen. I guess she’s been testing them out on unlucky Maelstromers for fun.

REGINA: I wonder if she’s still on her vendetta after all that happened with the Tyger Claws and her brother... anyways, thanks for checking that out. I’ll need the blades, so send them my way via the nearest drop point.
