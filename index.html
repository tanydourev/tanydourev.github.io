HELLO!

I'm Tany, a Bulgarian-Canadian multimedia designer. I specialize in graphic & visual design, 3D design and creative prototyping through iteration. Much of my work is inspired by video games, neo-futuristic aesthetics and cyberpunk themes. I like to tell stories through my designs and bring people to new worlds. Emotions are a powerful tool and I use them to my advantage. Simple is always best, but I won't say no to a little chaos here and there.
