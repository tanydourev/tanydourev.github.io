meet the designer.
I'm a Bulgarian-Canadian multimedia designer with a focus on graphic/visual design and 3D projects. I like to approach my work from different perspectives and exploring the paths less taken by industry experts. Video games and anything cyberpunk are my greatest muses – from the colours and aesthetics to the smallest of pixels. They say being a jack of all trades equates to a master of none, but I enjoy dabbling in different fields to see what interests me.

On a regular day, you can find me in Brock University's infamous "H Lab", where I'm most likely working on a project or researching something to do with Cyberpunk 2077.
