Meet Tany

Hello there! I’m an interaction designer, gamer and type nerd based in Canada. My creative journey began with graphic/visual design, and has since expanded into typography, UX/UI design, 3D and game design. Much of my work is inspired by cyberpunk aesthetics, futuristic art and video games.

I currently work as a Graphic Designer @ RW Digitial Marketing Agency. Outside of design, I enjoy a good cup of tea, listening to video game soundtracks and watching Star Wars ✨
