The Witch's Brew tea

The Witch's Brew is a tea brand inspired by astrology, witchcraft and spirituality. It encourages drinkers to embrace the power of the cosmos and combine it with the herbal goodness of tea, turning it into positive energy that brings about balance. I aimed to integrate a playful and light-hearted nature in this brand, unravelling the mystery of the universe and making it approachable to those who aren't as familiar with astrology.

In addition to the standard lineup, The Witch's Brew also features a collection of teas based on the twelve signs of the zodiac, called the Zodiac Collection. Each sign is assigned a type of tea that corresponds to the nature of their element (water, earth, fire, air). The packaging for these teas is different as to help consumers identify the difference between the brand's regular teas and the specialty collection.
