Branding & Logo Design
Kent St.Claire is a branding concept for a designer menswear company. KS.C, for short, aims to connect luxury with comfort, using high-quality fabrics and materials to manufacture products that last a lifetime. Inspired by the monogram style of logos employed by brands such as Dolce & Gabbana (D&C), Louis Vuitton (LV) and Jimmy Choo (JC), KS.C emphasizes traditional high-fashion culture through the modified version of Roxborough CF's serif typeface. Kross Neue Grotesk provides a sans-serif counterpart to represent both the brand name and the location, both of which vary depending on the selected logo combination. KS.C's circular logo draws inspiration from Versace's Medusa token.
