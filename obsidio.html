OBSIDIO

2021 / 3D Modelling / Rhino 7, Chaos V-Ray, Blender

OBSIDIO is a cover re-design of the third installment in the Illuminae Files series by Amie Kaufman and Jay Kristoff. It combines a fresh interpretation of the book's events with literary devices that emphasize key components of the plot. Subtle references to the formatting of the book and its setting are also present.

01/ PROCESS

Designing the cover was split into a process of two steps: create a list of literary devices that represent elements of the book's plot; implement them in a visual format with an accompanying grid system. Given the events of the third installment are related to the previous books in the series, there are several connections that can be made solely through the visual elements of the cover.

The following are examples of literary devices used and their respective visual counterparts:

ALLUSIONS
(1) The LCD typeface represents the formatting of chapters through reports, screens and messages.
(2) Glow effects and screen lines represent the presence of technology and artificial intelligence systems.
(3) The cursor line at the end of OBISIDIO is a reference to a command line application (ex. WIN+R on Windows).
(4) The text in the background is a report of AIDAN's system errors and a summary of its disobedience against direct orders, which is an essential component of the plot.

METAPHORS
(1) The liquified text is a visual representation of the chaos that all characters, ships and planets experience throughout the series.
(2) The liquified text also represents radio waves and communication, which are often scrambled amidst various areas of space.
