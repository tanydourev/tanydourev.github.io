Voxel Art
The MK Voxel series is a passion project that explores the world of voxel art through the lens of Mario Kart Tour city tracks. Designed to simulate a "world in a box", each piece highlights a portion of the track that is a landmark or scenic view. The style of MagicaVoxel allows for creative takes on 3D objects and forms such as these, mimicking the infamous Minecraft block appearance.

TOKYO BLUR :
The Tokyo Blur track features the Asakuna Kannon Temple (also known as Sensō-ji) in Tokyo. As one of the oldest temples in the city, it was built to honour the goddess of Kannon and completed in the year 645. Nintendo pays tribute to this temple by displaying it in the middle section of the track, with players driving under the giant red lantern that hangs under the center of the gate.
↓
PARIS PROMENADE :
The Paris Promenade track features the Jardins du Trocadéro (Trocadéro Gardens) leading up to the Pont d'léna bridge and the Eiffel Tower. This is located on the northern side of the Seine River, which players drive through after exiting the narrow streets of Paris.
↓
AMSTERDAM DRIFT :
The Amsterdam Drift track features a combination of in-city driving and rural paths alongside the Zaan river. The scenic windmills, shown below, are a reference to the Zaanse Schanse neighbourhood near Zaandijk. Positioned along the banks of the Zaan, players approach this winding section of the track after passing through a glider segment.
