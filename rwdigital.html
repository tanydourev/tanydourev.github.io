RW Digital design & branding

↓

In 2021, I joined the amazing team at RW Digitial Marketing Agency as a Graphic Designer who focuses primarily on managing the agency's social media feeds and working on client websites. I create graphics for services the agency provides, educational tutorials and trend updates within the field of digital marketing, client project showcases, and posts that highlight events in the Richmond community. Additionally, I reinforce the agency's branding standards through the content I create and collaborate directly with the agency Principal to continuously re-evalute our marketing efforts.

Social media content

A major component of my work with RW Digital involves designing graphics for social media usage, specifically Instagram, LinkedIn and the agency's blog. Through these posts, the agency aims to educate those interested in digital marketing by providing tutorials and updates on current trends, analytics tools, and strategies for improved website performance. The graphics I create are intended to simplify digital marketing topics for viewers and encourage a friendly approach to the complex field of analytics.

Apart from digital marketing content, I also create graphics for events in the Richmond community, promotional content for client projects, and celebratory posts for holidays. RW Digital is proud to acknowledge the Asian community in Richmond, sharing cultural traditions and celebrations that take place within the region through social media. I have the priviledge of working on posts for annual Chinese holidays, such as the Lunar New Year and Mid-Autumn Festival.

Client websites

Collaborating alongside the agency Principal, I have worked on several client websites and continue to maintain them when changes are proposed from agency clients. My responsibilities include defining the visual style of the websites based on the client branding, ensuring the validity of website content and suggesting updates where necessary, and improving user experiences through responsive design for screens.

Agency branding

RW Digital's branding emphasizes a technical, yet approachable style that communicates the agency's interest in helping its clients feel empowered by the tools it can provide. Graphics for social media contain visual elements such as flowing gradients and organic shapes, combinations of circles and grid lines, and stroke-based linework. While the agency has a pre-existing colour scheme for its branding, we have experimented with various approaches to combining colour with Instagram's grid layout for account pages. This has allowed the agency to diversify its feed from the style of previous content and better solidify its branding guidelines.

In addition to defining branding standards for social media, the agency also received an upgraded website and business card design for the agency Principal.
